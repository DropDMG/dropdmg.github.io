How It Works

Step 1: Drag and Drop

Simply drag a folder, file, or existing disk image onto the DropDMG window or Dock icon.

Step 2: Configure Options

Select encryption, segmentation, layout, or compression settings from the intuitive interface.

Step 3: Create or Convert

DropDMG generates the disk image instantly. Convert formats or burn to disc in one step.

Step 4: Access and Share

Mount the image in Finder for direct access. Ready for distribution or backup.
